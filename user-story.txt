Requirements:

System Administrator:
Verify registered NGOs based on documents submitted. (Latest Tax document, Company registration), name, location.
Blacklist NGOs, student accounts.

Program Admin/NGO
Can create an organization account to manage fund distribution programs using valid documents to verify their organization.
Can create scholarship programs, for eg. “Girls Education Program 2026” with details.
Define scholarship categories (tuition, books, uniforms, stationery).
Register scholarship students, attach guardian details and school information.
Generate a digital scholarship ID for the child and assign a scholarship wallet to the student.
Approve incoming invoices, approve verified vendors. 
Create receipts, Add supporting documents.
Redirect funds that have been allocated to NGOs to individual students or programs.
View individual student applications for their programs and accept/reject applications.

Schools:
Schools can register themselves as a partner to accept scholarship fundings.
Schools can send invoices to claim funds for tuition, uniform, food, etc. (optional)

Donor
Browse verified NGOs, scholarship programs, individual list of students.
Donate funds to support children’s education.
See fund distribution after donation - a transparent record of allocation.

Students:
Apply for an individual scholarship program.

As a public viewer, I want to see the total number of scholarships funded and high level spending flows -  to verify that funds were used properly.
